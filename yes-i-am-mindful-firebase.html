Admin Help

1

Sign in with Google

Use your Google account to sign in as admin. No passwords. If you see "Access Denied", ask your technical contact to add your Google account to the admins list in Firestore.

2

Create a Batch

Batches → Fill name, start date, institution → Create Batch. Participants select this at signup.

3

Upload Photos

In Weekly Themes or Tasks → click the upload zone → select a photo from your device. It saves automatically to Firebase Storage.

4

Advance Tasks Daily

Batches → click your batch → Batch Control → click "Advance Task" each day to unlock the next task for participants.

5

All Data is Automatic

There is no Sheet URL to paste. Firebase handles everything. Data appears for all participants immediately after you save.
